External chatacters of DWPI Mincho, Nishiki teki, Mkana+, Jigmo

these are notation for display external chatacters of DWPI Mincho, Nishiki teki, Mkana+, Jigmo,

  • DWPI Mincho: &dw-пҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯ;
  • Nishiki teki: &ni-пҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯ;
  • Mkana+: &mk-пҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯ;
  • Jigmo: &jg-пҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯ;
  • Jigmo 2: &j2-пҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯ;
  • Jigmo 3: &j3-пҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯпҝӯ;

Note: External characters of DWPI Mincho is disabled by config default. DWPI Mincho can't convert web font, so you have to install this.
